Mediathread is an innovative platform built by the Columbia CTL that supports collaborative multimedia analysis.

For Instructors

Engage students and track their work with a range of innovative assignments.

For Students

Back up insights with targeted citation of multimedia source materials.

For Researchers

Collaborate across digital collections with shared annotations, tagging, and controlled vocabularies.

Users of Mediathread are able to lift video, audio, and image items from a range of digital collections and then work with them in a course-specific website.
